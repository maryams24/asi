Practical 10
Compliance Audit Simulation:
✅ Step 1: Create a Folder for Audit Files
mkdir C:\Compliance_Audit
cd C:\Compliance_Audit


________________


✅ Step 2: System Information Audit
systeminfo > system_info.txt


✔ Stores complete system details.
________________


✅ Step 3: User Accounts Audit
net user > users_list.txt


For detailed info of all users:
wmic useraccount get name,sid,status > user_details.txt


________________


✅ Step 4: Password Policy Check
net accounts > password_policy.txt


________________


✅ Step 5: Running Services Audit
sc query state= all > services_list.txt


________________


✅ Step 6: Installed Software Audit
wmic product get name,version > installed_software.txt


________________


✅ Step 7: Firewall Status Check
netsh advfirewall show allprofiles > firewall_status.txt


________________


✅ Step 8: Open Ports & Network Connections
netstat -ano > open_ports.txt


________________


✅ Step 9: Group Policy Result
gpresult /r > group_policy.txt


________________


✅ Step 10: Combine All Results into One File (Optional)
copy *.txt final_compliance_report.txt
